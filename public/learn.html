🌱 Farmer Learning Centre

🌾 Identifying Fungal Diseases

Look for white or gray powdery patches, yellowing, and curling leaves. Often caused by humidity and lack of airflow.

🐛 Detecting Pest Attacks

Check for holes, sticky leaves, and slow growth. Leaf curl, droppings, or wilting are also signs of infestation.

💧 Watering Techniques

Water early morning or late evening. Avoid wetting the leaves to prevent fungal growth.

🍅 Common Tomato Issues

Blight, mosaic virus, and root rot are common. Ensure proper spacing and avoid overwatering.

🧪 Organic Remedies

Use neem oil, garlic-chili sprays, or cow urine-based mixes for pest control and fungal prevention.
